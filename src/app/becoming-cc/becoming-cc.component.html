How to become Content Creator ?

Hey Void Players !

If you wanna join our Content Creator Crew, open a ticket via ticket-system on NosVoid discord – and of course, read following informations to know how everything’s working.

Our reward system includes 6 ranks to reach

  • Bronze Content Creator
  • Silver Content Creator
  • Gold Content Creator
  • Platinum Content Creator
  • Diamond Content Creator
  • Master Content Creator

Master Content Creator will only be given to one person each (Youtube + Twitch).

Views system

For YouTube channels

  • 150 – 299 monthly views for Bronze Content Creator
  • 300 – 499 monthly views for Silver Content Creator
  • 500 – 1499 monthly views for Gold Content Creator
  • 1500 – 2499 monthly views for Platinum Content Creator
  • 2500 – 4999 monthly views for Diamond Content Creator
  • 5000 + monthly views for Master Content Creator

For Twitch channels

  • 50 – 199 monthly viewers for Bronze Content Creator
  • 200 – 399 monthly viewers for Silver Content Creator
  • 400 – 699 monthly viewers for Gold Content Creator
  • 700 – 1099 monthly viewers for Platinum Content Creator
  • 1100 – 1599 monthly viewers for Diamond Content Creator
  • 1600 + monthly viewers for Master Content Creator

Views will be counted on 5th of every month. That means that we will give ranks depending on views gained in current month.

The Rules

  • - Content Creators need to upload at least 5 videos or make 5 streams in each month.
  • - Rules of NosVoid should be applied in all contents (if Content Creators break rules on their own, we may remove them from Content Creator Crew)
  • - If Content Creators doing any content for other servers, it shouldn’t be related with NosVoid (comparisons between NosVoid and different servers are forbidden).
  • - Content Creators should send links of their videos or streams into special video channel on NosVoid discord.

The Rewards

  • - In-Game-Title for every rank until Gold + Master. Platinum and Diamond can’t be given.
  • - Special role and chat on void discord (role depending on reached rank).
  • - Special ingame name tag depending on reached rank ([BCC] - [SCC] - [GCC] - [PCC] - [DCC] - [MCC]).
  • - Monthly coin prizes (amount depends on reached rank and will be send 5th of every month).
    (If you don’t have 5 videos in a month your coins will be calculated with following formula: rank x coins x (video amount / 5)
  • - Monthly coupons to make giveaways for viewers of videos and stream.
