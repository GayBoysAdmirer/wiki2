Carve Runes - weapons

How to get your Carve Runes ?

To get your Carve Runes, you can upgrade your Rune at Carve Rune NPC in the Tattoo & Rune Map in NosVille or you can use scrolls.


There are two different scrolls : Rune of Fortune Scroll & Premium Rune of Fortune Scroll. Both scrolls prevent the weapon from being damaged and might save your materials while upgrading. The Premium one adds 2% chance of success.

Carve Runes Materials (Weapon)


Which options can you get ?

  • - All attacks are increased by 20 (250 max) VERY GOOD
  • - All attacks are increased by 1%. (13% max) MUST HAVE
  • - Your specialist's elemental skill points are increased by 1 (6 max) VERY GOOD PVE
  • - Your specialist's attack skill points are increased by 1 (6 max) MUST HAVE AT LEAST 3
  • - The equipped fairy's element increases by 3. (20 max) GOOD PVE
  • - Damage is increased to Dragon 3%. (20% max) BONUS PVE (P9 RAIDS)
  • - Increases damage from critical hits by 3% (25% max) BONUS
  • - Chance of inflicting critical hits is increased by 1% (10% max) VERY GOOD - NO MAGE
  • - Damage in PvE is increased by 3% (20% max) BAD
  • - Concentration is increased by 1 during the magic attack. (22 max) BAD
  • - All element energies are increased by 10.(60 max) BAD

Which powers can you get ?

Note that each power becomes "strong" at Lv 5 & 6. Before, they are only "small bonuses" on which you can't really count on. You can get a power or a power Lv every 3 Rune Lv (3 - 6 - 9 - 12 - 15 - 18 - 21).

Carve Runes - armours

Carve Runes Materials (Armour)


Which options can you get ?

  • - All elemental resistance is increased by 3 (20 max) BAD
  • - All defence powers are increased by 50 (300 max) VERY GOOD
  • - Defence is increased by 3% (18% max) MUST HAVE
  • - Your specialist's defence skill points are increased by 1 (5 max) GOOD
  • - Your specialist's HP/MP skill points are increased by 1 (5 max) VERY GOOD
  • - With a 100% probability all elemental damage is reduced by 2% (25% max) BAD
  • - Damage from critical hits is reduced by 4% (25% max) VERY GOOD
  • - Probability to receive critical hits is decreased by 1% (12% max) GOOD

Which powers can you get ?

There isn't "MUST HAVE" powers, it depends of the way you want to play. Note that each power becomes "strong" at Lv 5 & 6. Before, they are only "small bonuses" on which you can't really count on. You can get a power or a power Lv every 3 Rune Lv (3 - 6 - 9 - 12 - 15 - 18 - 21).
