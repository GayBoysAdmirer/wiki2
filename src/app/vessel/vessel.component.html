What is Sealed Vessel Event?



OPEN    SEALED VESSEL TO RELEASE MONSTERS IN ANY MAP (EXCEPT SPECIAL MAPS).

KILL MINI BOSSES FROM SEALED VESSEL TO RECEIVE    SEALED TREASURE CHEST (100% CHANCE)
& SEALED VESSEL'S SPECIAL BOX. (7% CHANCE)

REMEMBER, THE PERSON WHO DOES LAST HIT TO THE MINI BOSSES WILL RECEIVE THE REWARD. SO WE SUGGEST YOU TO DON'T OPEN IN PUBLIC AREAS.

How we can craft Sealed Vessel?



YOU CAN FIND CRAFT NPC IN NOSVILLE, NEAR STARTER PACK NPC.
YOU WILL NEED ICE SOUL (1X) & SHINY HAT (100X) TO CRAFT SEALED VESSEL.

ICE SOUL : OBTAINABLE FROM ACT4 RAIDS AND CALIGOR. (100% CHANCE TO GET AFTER COMPLETING).
SHINY HAT : OBTAINABLE FROM TUNDRA, FROST LAND AND UNKNOWN LAND MONSTERS.
