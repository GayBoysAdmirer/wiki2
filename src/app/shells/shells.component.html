Betting

You can bet your eqs at Betting NPC, top left of NosVille

There are two type of amulets :

Champion Blessing Amulet (Random)

Change the Rarity and the Shell

Champion Blessing Amulet

Change ONLY the Rarity

Autobet

You can get it for 999 coins in VoidMall. Once you equiped it, you can't trade it anymore, it's linked to your character. The autobet works only with red amulets. After wearing the Autobet, you need to fill your inventory with red amulets, while betting the Autobet will use the red amulets in your inventory to bet until you get R7 or R8.
