How to get a shell ?

Betting

You can bet your eqs at Betting NPC, top left of NosVille

To bet non-champion eqs you need to buy

There are two type of amulets :

Red Amulets

Change the Rarity and the Shell

Grey Amulets

Change the Rarity but not the Shell

Autobet

You can get it for 999 coins in VoidMall. Once you equiped it, you can't trade it anymore, it's linked to your character. The autobet works only with red amulets. After wearing the Autobet, you need to fill your inventory with red amulets, while betting the Autobet will use the red amulets in your inventory to bet until you get R7 or R8.

Upgrading +16 to +20

Upgrading from +16 to +20 works the same way except that it requires a new scroll and a new material.

You can obtain 20x Dragon Card Protection Scrolls after completing an Act4 raid.
The new material is Dragon Gem. You can craft them with Blood-Red Rubies at Dragon Gem NPC in Act4 Citadel (1x Blood-Red Ruby gives 5x Dragon Gems).
