Informations

  • - You will get quests by one by. (For example you will get Participate in the Instant Combat first, next quest will appear once you are done with current one!)
  • - You have a specific time to do quests, like you have 4 days to complete level 1 quests. But even if you complete all from first day, you need to wait til last day to get next level quest.
  • - If you are not done with your level quest, it will be replaced with next level quests and you won't receive any rewards from the quests you didn't complete.
  • - You can't have the next following quests if you didn't succeeded the previous one !
