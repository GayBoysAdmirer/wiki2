-- Land of Living --

Land of Living is a family map for levelling, this is the only map you will use to reach P9 Level 99 (+70).
Each Family will have access to their own LoL without disruption of outside players, and you can access this map on every Channel.
Grouping with players will NOT reduce experience gain.


Drops
· All monsters will drop Gold and a small chance for Dragon Health Potion.
· Mini-Boss Magic Undead Dragon will be the only monster that can drop Magic Undead Fang.




-- God of Void --

God of Void is a Story Line available from Prestige 9 Level 99 (+50), you will be asked to continue your story with the god of your choice.
Each with a different reward and new permanent buff that will suit your playstyle.
Choose wisely as you won't be able to change your god again. (But you will be able to switch buff via item from Item Shop in the future.)
Everything about each god can be found in Mysterious Traveller NPC.

Rewards:
Upon completing 5 Quests of your chosen god, you will receive their Permanent Buff.
If you complete the 6th quest you will receive a SP10 of your class as a reward.


God of Magicians

  • · All enemies resistances is decreased by 10.
  • · Damage in Raids is increased by 10%.
  • · 5% to reset skill CD on hit.

God of Demons

  • · All Attacks Increased by 5%.
  • · Dodge Increased by 100.
  • · Crit dmg increased by 10%.

God of Angels

  • · Crit chance increased by 4%.
  • · Hitrate increased by 150.
  • · Crit dmg taken is decreased by 10%.

God of Disaster

  • · All Defences are increased by 5%.
  • · Dodge is increased by 120.
  • · Antimalus 10%.



-- Asgobas Instant Combat --

During Asgobas IC you will face Dragon Lord Asgobas that will Retreat every time his HP Bar is reduced by 50%.
The Asgobas Instant Combat ends when Max HP of Dragon Lord Asgobas reaches 50%.

  • Every time Asgobas returns to the ground he will spawn (near him):
  • · Giant Lava Dragon
  • · Asgobas Child
  • · Dragon Army's Dragon

During 2nd and 4th Retreat a new mini boss Golden Undead Dragon will spawn.
That will make Dragon Lord Asgobas almost immortal.
You need to kill him to get rid of the buff inflicted on Asgobas.

  • Every minute the Golden Undead Dragon is alive, will use his special skill that will spawn Blessed Dragons.
  • IMPORTANT: Only Dragon Health Potion works in Asgobas IC. All Other potions can't be used!

-- Power of The Weapons --

What is Draconic Power
Draconic Power is an item to further perfect your weapons, there are 2 types of Draconic Powers,
PvP and PvE and are only usable on primary and secondary weapons

PvE

Where to Craft:
Prestige 9 Special Items NPC

PvP

Materials for crafting:
· 5x Angelic Horn
· 5x Vengeful Draco Horn

If you want to change Draconic Power that is currently in the weapon,
you will need to use the Change Draconic Power item available ONLY from the store.




-- Armor Carve --

Armor Carve Runes work the same as Weapon Carve Runes but the only difference
is the materials that are needed to upgrade.
You can upgrade the carve on armor with both:
Rune of Fortune Scroll and Premium Rune of Fortune Scroll.


Materials Needed

  • Dragon Soul
  • From: World Bosses / P9 Daily Box

  • Runic Dragon Powder
  • From: World Bosses / P9 Daily Box

  • Dratanium
  • From: As drop on Asgobas IC and P9 Weekly Quest

  • Ice Dragon Energy
  • From: Asgobas' IC box

  • Fire Dragon Energy
  • From: Asgobas' IC box

  • Moonlight Dragon Energy
  • From: Asgobas' IC box

  • Sky Dragon Energy
  • From: Asgobas' IC box

  • Ancient Dragon Runestone
  • From: P9 Weekly Quest

  • Corrupted Celestial Spire Fragment
  • From: Carve Rune Box 1



    • Available Bonuses
    · All elemental resistance is increased by 3 (Max 20)
    · All defence powers are increased by 50 (Max 300).
    · Defence is increased by 3% (Max 18%).
    · Your specialist's defence skill points are increased by 1 (Max 5).
    · Increases maximum HP by 1% (Max 13%).
    · Your specialist's HP/MP skill points are increased by 1 (Max 5).
    · Increases maximum MP by 1% (Max 13%).
    · With a 100% probability all elemental damage is reduced by 2% (Max 25%).
    · Damage from critical hits is reduced by 4% (Max 25%).
    · Probability to receive critical hits is decreased by 1% (Max 12%).
