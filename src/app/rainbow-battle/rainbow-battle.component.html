Mechanics & Ranks

Mechanics

  • - Changed duration of Rainbow Battle to 7 minutes.
  • - Rainbow Battle is 5v5 from now on.
  • - Crystals giving more points than before.

Ranks

  • - 6 wins to reach Bronze
  • - 12 wins to reach Silver
  • - 18 wins to reach Gold
  • - 25 wins to reach Platinum
  • - 40 wins to reach Diamond

Diamonds also get extra points.
At day 30 (reset) Top 1-2-3 points get the rewards.
Points will be based on wins and kills.

Rewards

  • Top 1 : "Best Rainbow Player" title & Arena Wings.
  • Top 2 : "Rainbow God" title & Arena Wings.
  • Top 3 : "The Legend is Here" title & Arena Wings.
