World Bosses

Prestige 4 World Boss

At 16:00 and 22:45 the World Boss spawns at World Boss Area $warp p4. You need to kill the World Boss to get Tattoo and Carve Boxes

Act 4 World Boss

At 16:15 and 23:00 a portal spawn in Act4 Citadel.
First, Angels and Demons need to kill the World Boss.
Second, Angels and Demons will fight. The first side that reaches 300 kills wins.
At the end, everyone get :

  • - 2x Tattoo Box 1
  • - 2x Tattoo Box 2
  • - 2x Tattoo Box 3
  • - 2x Carve Runes Box 1
  • - 2x Carve Runes Box 2

Winner and Loser Side Boxes :

The chance to get items change between Winner and Loser Side Boxes.

  • - 1x Costume Piece
  • - 1x Change Skin 2
  • - 2x Rarify Item
  • - 1x Cold Heart

Prestige 8.1 World Boss

At 16:40 and 01:00 the World Boss spawns at World Boss Area $warp p8.1. This world boss works with 3 rounds in which Strange Chests will spawn everywhere on the map, you need to destroy them all.
