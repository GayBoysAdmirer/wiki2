World Bosses

Fire God Grenigas World Boss

At 16:00 and 22:45 the World Boss spawns at World Boss Area $warp wb. You need to kill the World Boss to get Tattoo and Carve Boxes

Act 4 World Boss

At 16:15 and 23:00 a portal spawn in Act4 Citadel.
First, Angels and Demons need to kill the World Boss.
Second, Angels and Demons will fight. The first side that reaches 300 kills wins.
At the end, everyone get :

  • - 2x Tattoo Box 1
  • - 2x Tattoo Box 2
  • - 2x Tattoo Box 3
  • - 2x Carve Runes Box 1
  • - 2x Carve Runes Box 2

Winner and Loser Side Boxes :

The chance to get items change between Winner and Loser Side Boxes.

  • - 1x Costume Piece
  • - 1x Change Skin 2
  • - 2x Rarify Item
  • - 1x Cold Heart

Volcano World Boss

At 20:00 the World Boss spawns at World Boss Area $warp wb Double-Instanced World boss, defeat minibosses to obtain items of great value. After defeating minibosses, a World Boss will spawn.
