Soul System

Gather the souls of various Raidbosses in order to craft valuable items.
They will be obtainable in Raidboxes.
Souls will be obtainable only from following Raidbosses :

  • - Lord Draco
  • - Glacerus, the Ice cold
  • - Laurena
  • - Zenas
  • - Erenia
  • - Kirollas
  • - Pure Beast

Collect all of them to craft Badge Evolution.
After completing each raid, you will get a certain amount of Raid Tickets. You will need them for crafting Minor and Major Rewards

Draco, Glacerus, Laurena, Maru, Zenas, Erenia, Fernon, Kirollas, Pure Beast : 1x Raid Ticket.
Paimon, Belial, Sandstorm, Grenigas : 2x Raid Ticket.
Hardcore, Ibrahim,Kertos : 4x Raid Ticket.

What is Badge Evolution ?

It is an item that evolves your badge and grants you 2 new extra stats to help you on your journey.
You can get following stats :

  • - Attack/Defence/Property perfections are increased 2, 4, 6, 8, 10
  • - Increase attack 3 / 5 / 8%
  • - Increase defence 3 / 5 / 8%
  • - Icrease damage inside Raids 5 / 8 / 12%
  • - Increase cooldown reduction 4 / 8 / 12%

PS: You can change additional effects by using Evolution Badge Rarify Changer.

Pity System for Souls

Pity System has been implemented for following raid boxes: Draco, Glacerus, Zenas, Erenia, Carno, Kirollas and Laurena.

Purpose of Pity System is to help our newbie players to farm Badge Evolution items quicker! There will be counter of 500 boxes where you get a guaranteed reward when you reach 500 box.
