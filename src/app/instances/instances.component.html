Private Instances

--What is Private Instances? --

Private Instances is brand new system where it lets you create ow maps for yourself or with your group members!
With that new system you can farm as much as you want without being bothered by other players.

--Where can i find entry? --

You can enter Private Instance on every exp map.
Goth Elder NPC will be waiting for you at the entrance, which allows you to create a instance.



Now is to choose whether you want to enter alone or with a group and enjoy the peace of mind while leveling!
