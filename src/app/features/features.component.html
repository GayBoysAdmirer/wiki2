Features

Discord RPC

You can show your NosVoid activity on your Discord.

FPS Unlock

The FPS Unlock feature allows you to "unlock" the Nostale frame rate, increasing it to above the default of 60 FPS which may reduce the input lag and allow players to make the most out of monitors with a 60hz+ refresh rate which will make NosTale so much more enjoyable for players!

CD on Skills

The CD on skills will have a new feature, the bar will show the seconds missing for the skill to be avaiable again. Are your friends asking how many seconds are missing for your buff? Now you know!

More Tattoos Slots

Now you can have 4 instead of 2 tattoos upgraded. You can switch between them by using button to change slots.

Tattoos & Carves Upgrader

Now you can upgrade tattoos and runes much faster. Choose from 1 to 25 upgrades with just one click.

Switch Equipments

You will be able to save 5 equipment sets at the same time and of course use any of them quickly.

Fast Craft

You will be able to craft all the stuff much faster, instead of waiting for hours to craft some things.

Reworked Friend Chat

Refreshed and improved friend-chat now allows you to invite to a group or trade directly from the chat window

Add Old Raid Team Button

After using a raid seal, you can add the whole team just by pressing this button (close to the Mini-Map).

Auto Buff Button

You can use that button to buy buffs. Buffs lasts 15min and costs 12.5kk.

Wings Swapper

It's a new feature where it lets you change your specialist' wings, but keeps the buff of original wings of your specialist card. Difference of between old Change Wings and new Wing Swapper is that we don't need to use commands with new system. We also have made that, you need to use Swappers of the wings to unlock them on your Wing Swapper. Why is that? Because we will be releasing Visual Wings collections in the future, that will help us to implement them better. You can buy Wing Swapper Pack from the NosVoid shop for 2000 coins. All you need is double click on Wing Swappers to unlock them on your Wing Swapper Window.

Event Schedule

Now you can see upcoming Server Events timers more precisely! Click on calendar icon to open the events window.

Warp Button

You can use that button for fast travel.

The Bidding System

It can be found on the Website in Nosbazar tab.

It will be easy to sell & buy stuff.

  • → You are be able to set options as you want in search button to find the stuff you want. (Like shell options, SP sums...)
  • → And add your stuff in auction list from your inventory witch just using website.
  • → Choose the item you want to sell, set the time - offer (if you're open to offers) - max price...
  • → And you will receive your offers - who wants to buy your stuffs!

All will be on the Administration page !
