Soul System

What is Soul System?

Soul System is a system that introduces the Souls of Raid Bosses in the form of an item.
You can get them from the box of a given raid.
You can craft valuable items from Souls.
The entrance to the Soul System is at $warp p4.

You can get souls only from these Raid Bosses:

Lord Draco
Glacerus    
Laurena    
Kirollas    
Pure Beast



Raid Ticket



After completing each raid, you will get a certain amount of Raid Tickets.
You will need them for crafting Minor and Major Rewards.
You can get Raid Tickets from:

Draco, Glacerus, Laurena, Maru, Fernon, Kirollas, Pure Beast (1x Raid Ticket)
 Paimon, Belial, Sandstorm, Grenigas (2x Raid Ticket)
 Hardcore, Ibrahim,Kertos (4x Raid Ticket)

Pity System


Pity System has been implemented for Soul System.
Purpose of Pity System is to help our players to farm Souls quicker!
There will be counter of 500 boxes where you get a guaranteed reward when you reach 500 box.
Pity System works for following raid boxes: Draco, Glacerus, Carno, Kirollas, Laurena.
