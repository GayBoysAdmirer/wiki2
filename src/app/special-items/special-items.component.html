Tattoo Change

Use this crystal to change your tattoo loa. Switch loa to receive a random skill. The new skill's level will be altered to match the level of your previous skill. You can use that item on all tattoo levels.

Tattoo Switch Scroll

Use this scroll to select a new tattoo skill from your loa. Once you've confirmed which new skill you want, its level will be adjusted to match the level of the skill you were previously using. You can use that item on all tattoo levels.
